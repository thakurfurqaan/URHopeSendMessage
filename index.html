The COVID-19 Pandemic has hugely affected the world, with the underprivileged section bearing its brunt the most. In this time of crisis, a lot of people have come forward to help these worst affected sections. 


 We, alumni and students from IIT Bombay as well as other institutes have also banded together to do our bit by developing an interactive website www.urhope.in which can accelerate support to the ones in need.


 Check our website as well as the profiles of team members for further details at Urhope.in/team 


  How  does URHope help people?


 - Hyperlocal information (of all states and UTs) about govt./NGO/Community initiatives regarding food, shelter, hospitals, testing centre, e-passes, helplines etc. 


 - Connecting distress calls to ground volunteers coming from NGOs/Community organizations providing relief. 


 -Support system  in post pandemic rehabilitation
